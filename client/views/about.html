About

You know when you are preparing for an exam by doing exams from previous years? Noticed how boring it is do do the multiple choice questions on paper or PDF, having to check the answers on another paper/PDF afterwards?

Kramster helps making this a little more fun. Select your school and course, and cram for your exam by doing the multiple choice questions with instant feedback!

To keep Kramster up to date, your help is needed. Do you have any exams that are not on Kramster? Please contribute.

Kramster is made by Mats Byrkjeland, computer science student at NTNU. Kramster is open source and has a free, open API
