Contribute

In the future this page will contain a sexy form, making you able to add your own exams and questions. But this is coming later. Therefore, if you want to contribute to the database, you can do this by sending your question documents (exams) per email, and I will add it for you. What a great way to help your fellow students, don't you think?

If you're a hero, you can send me a .txt on the following format:

The CorrectAnswerIndices comprise the array of of indexes of the correct answers in the following list of options. These are split by spaces.

School
Course
Name
Question1 §§ CorrectAnswerIndex1 CorrectAnswerIndex2 ... CorrectAnswerIndexN §§ Option1 §§ Option1 §§ ... §§ OptionN
Question2 §§ CorrectAnswerIndex1 CorrectAnswerIndex2 ... CorrectAnswerIndexN §§ Option1 §§ Option1 §§ ... §§ OptionN
...
QuestionN §§ CorrectAnswerIndex1 CorrectAnswerIndex2 ... CorrectAnswerIndexN §§ Option1 §§ Option1 §§ ... §§ OptionN

Example:

University of Foo, San Bar
FOO1337 Foobarian Algebra
Exam Spring 2014
What is foo? §§ 0 §§ Bar §§ Bob §§ Alice §§ Dijkstra
What is bar? §§ 0 1 §§ Pub §§ Foo §§ Chocolate
